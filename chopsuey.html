Nani's Chop Suey

simple and easy


This recipe was made by Hoomowatee Seesaran


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup carrot
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup mushroom
  • 1 teaspoon MSG
  • 2-3 spoon soy sauce
  • 2 spring onion cut
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 spoon cornflower
  • 1-2 block of tofu in cubes
  • 1/2 cup courgette
  • 1 pepper

Method

  1. sauté the vegetables in very little oil and take out. Sauté the courgette without salt.
  2. Put one spoon on oil in a pan and scorch the garlic.
  3. Put your vegetables inside the pan with the garlic
  4. Add a cup of water
  5. Add a pinch of salt and leave to cook.
  6. As soon as vegetables are semi-cooked, add onions and cubed tofu.
  7. Add soy sauce, cornflower.
  8. leave to cook for 15 mins
  9. Take out if well cooked, or leave for 5 more mins if not fully cooked.
  10. Enjoy!
