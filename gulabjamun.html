Nani's Gulab Jamun

simple and easy


This recipe was made by Hoomowatee Seesaran


Ingredients

  • 1 tin condensed milk
  • cardamom
  • Gros Lani
  • 1 lb flour
  • 3 spoon ghee melted (melted but not cold)
  • 1 teaspoon bicarbonate

Method

  1. Mix milk and ghee, then bicarbonate, then add flour
  2. Mix until soft
  3. Fry in oil until light brown
  4. Put cold syrup
  5. Take out and let it rest
  6. When cool, enjoy!
