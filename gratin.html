Nani's Gratin

simple and easy


This recipe was made by Hoomowatee Seesaran


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of calabash (cut in cubes)
  • 1/2 onion (cut in thin slices)
  • 3/4 spoon of oil or butter
  • A touch of parsley
  • A touch of thyme
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 2 spoons of flour
  • 1 cup milk powder (diluted in water) (1 1/2 cup boiled calabash water)
  • 2 crushed biscuits
  • Salt
  • oil

Method

  1. Boil the calabash with 1 1/2 cups of water (add a pinch of salt)
  2. Stir the water of the boiled calabash to prepare the milk
  3. Crush the Calabash
  4. Put oil or butter to brown the onions then add thyme, parsley and calabash
  5. Let brown 2-3 min and pour the milk. Then, stir well.
  6. Pour into a buttered dish to put in the oven for 2-3 minutes
  7. Take out and enjoy
