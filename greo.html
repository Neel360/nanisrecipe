Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina
  • when frying use 1 cup ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Cardamon and gros lanille (crushed)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 spoons rasin and 4-5 almond
  • 1 cup milk powder mixed sugar
  • 3 spoons ghee

Method

  1. In a pot put ghee and scorch rasins, cardamom, gros lani and a bit of water.
  2. In the meantime, mix sugar, powder milk and greo
  3. When it starts to boil add little by little the greo mixture and almonds
  4. Take out later and let it rest
  5. When ready, enjoy!
